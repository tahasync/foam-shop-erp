Two bugs fixed in this pass

  • Inventory unit label: a product priced "/pc" now shows "7 pcs in stock" instead of "7.0 sq.ft in stock." The unit shown next to stock now follows the product's own pricing unit — added a second product below priced "/sq.ft" so you can see both rules holding at once.
  • Cash-in-hand / Baqaya card: the previous version cut a zigzag directly through the card's content using clip-path, which is what caused those overlapping green bars behind "Net Profit." Rebuilt so the torn edge is a separate strip drawn below the content — it can never overlap live data again — and the stat dividers are now simple border-lines instead of stretched 1px divs.

Signature idea — "the torn register slip"

Every other AI-generated ledger mockup reaches for a flat gradient hero card. Here, Cash in Hand and Khata Baqaya — the two numbers a shop owner actually tears off to hand someone or file away — are rendered as a real torn receipt: a jagged clip-path edge, a paper "stub" underneath with a slip number, drop-shadowed off the page. Regular content (products, transactions) stays on calm, clean cards — the torn edge is reserved for money, so it means something rather than decorating everything.

Supporting motifs

Dashed "stitch" dividers (nodding to foam/mattress quilting, not a generic hairline) mark section breaks and the low-stock line under inventory. Every card has a subtle top inner-highlight, like foam catching light at its bevel. The bottom nav is a floating stitched pill with a small amber thread-knot marking the active tab.

Icon system

All emoji (💰🧾📉👤 etc.) are replaced with a small custom line-icon set (grid, cart, box, users, wallet, receipt…) defined once as an SVG sprite and reused with <use>. This alone is one of the biggest "looks premium now" jumps — emoji render inconsistently across OS/fonts and read as a prototype, not a product.

Empty states

Since your real data only has one test product and no customers yet, Inventory and Khata show proper empty states — an icon, a plain-language explanation, a next action — instead of looking broken or unfinished.

Design tokens

Deep teal #0F6B64 primary · amber #E8A33D is the only non-brand accent (CTAs + the one low-stock alert) · Plus Jakarta Sans for headings/amounts, Inter for body/labels · tabular numerals on every currency figure · 16–20px card radius · dashed 2px stitch-lines instead of plain hairlines for section breaks · full dark mode with contrast-checked tokens carried over from v2.
