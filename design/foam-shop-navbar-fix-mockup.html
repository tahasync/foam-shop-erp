Design notes: In the broken version, the bottom 92px of the screen is a separate opaque surface (--surface block with a top border) that the pill merely sits on top of — this is what Scaffold.bottomNavigationBar paints by default, and what bottomNavigationBarTheme re-introduces even after removing an explicit Container color. In the correct version, that entire zone is transparent (pointer-events:none so it doesn't block taps on content behind it) and only the pill itself — a rounded card with its own background, border, and shadow — is visible. The scrollable content should be allowed to render behind the pill's transparent zone (extendBody: true), with enough bottom padding on the scroll view so real content never sits under the pill itself.
